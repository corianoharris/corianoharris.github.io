What is a design technologist?

"Think of a design technologist as a bridge between creativity and technology. Leveraging coding skills with research, design, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring designers' visions to life on the web. This enables rapid prototyping, iteration, and validation of design concepts, accelerating the development process."
