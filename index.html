Tools for success

Mindset

When I tackle a task or problem, I start by digging into the details to really understand what needs to be done. I break it down into smaller, manageable steps and set clear goals along the way. I prioritize what's most important and feasible, using tools like user research and design systems to guide me. Throughout the process, I keep everyone in the loop and gather feedback to refine my work. After implementing solutions, I thoroughly test everything to make sure it works well. It's all about finding creative solutions and improving as I go!

    Soft skills

  • Clearly explaining technical concept to a non-technical stakeholder.
  • Identifying bugs in code or user pain points then isolating them, and implementing a fix that improves performance without causing new issues.

  • Providing responsive and supportive service to resolve technical issues, ensuring a positive experience and building external and internal trust.
  • Learning a new programming language or framework to meet the evolving needs of a project, like switching from React to Angular or Adobe XD to Figma based on project requirements.
